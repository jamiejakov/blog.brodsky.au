![Kyari](images/Kyari.jpg)

Kyaaaaaaaryyyyyyyyy, one of the most '[unusual](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yzC4hFK5P3g)' J-Pop idols visited Sydney last weekend, and how better to celebrate your 6 month anniversary with your girlfriend, then by visiting this concert! It is the first time that Kyari visited Australia, and it seems that she rather likes it here! This one and a half hour concert featured all of her most popular songs, as well as some new and less famous ones. It was a very nice way to spend one of my last days in Sydney for 2014; together with my friends like [Seb](http://alonelyseptember.org/kyary-pamyu-pamyu-in-sydney/), [Ruben](http://rubenerd.com/kyary-pamyu-pamyu-in-sydney/), [Clara](http://kirinyan.net/farewell-and-kyary-pamyu-pamyu/), etc. I managed to take a lot of photos with Amy's iPhone and she posted them all up: [here](http://www.flickr.com/photos/dekophoto/sets/72157642820033415/).

Kyari is continuing her world tour now, and hopefully she will come to Sydney again next year! Better quality photos of the concert: [here](http://natalie.mu/music/gallery/show/news_id/112773/image_id/265530).
