Every year families all around the country gather together to celebrate [Australia Day](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Australia_Day). Its the day that the whole nation celebrates the anniversary of the 1788 arrival of the [First Fleet](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/First_Fleet 'First Fleet') of British Ships at Port Jackson, NSW. Australia has become a very multicultural society, but nevertheless everyone gathers together for festive barbecues and beach outings. All of this is usually followed by a display of fireworks in the CBD of the major cities.

Also, there is a antique car show! So Amy and I went to explore it. Old cars are so majestic, and the photos turned out great:
