I am collecting the Blu-rays for the Studio Ghibli movies. I love the studio and most of their movies, so I have set out to collect all of their BluRays! I mentioned [in a post](/posts/2013/ジブリがいっぱい-collection-2/) before as to how I started my collection, and now when Porco Rosso arrived I can blog about how my collection has grown.

_[Kiki](http://myanimelist.net/anime/512/Majo_no_Takkyuubin "Kiki's Delivery Service")_ was given to my for my birthday in February by my good friends from the anime club and _[Porco Rosso](http://myanimelist.net/anime/416/Porco_Rosso)_ I ordered myself, because it is my second favorite movie out of all the Ghiblies, so I had to have it! Lets see how long it will take for me to finish my collection.
