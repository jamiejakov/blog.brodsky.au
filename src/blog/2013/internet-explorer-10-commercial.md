The 90s! For me and most of my friends that was when we were born and were just growing up. I remember those times when I used to play with toy cars and Yo-Yos and when my first tamagochi died :( Trolls were these small creatures that everyone would have and omg the Pokemon Tazos!!! Those were the good old days, and this commercial made me remember them. Up until the last few seconds I felt really touched by this ad. I thought to myself, wow who made such a nice advertisement, its definitely a company that has to do with technology, but who? And then at the last scene I realized..... its just Microsoft trying to tell us that they "fixed" internet explorer and that now it is, apparently, usable... HA! Nope, not falling for that.
