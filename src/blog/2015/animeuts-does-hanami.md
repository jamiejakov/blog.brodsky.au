As per tradition, every year anime@UTS heads over to Auburn to experience the beauty of the Japanese sakura trees.  This year was no different. Unfortunately due to the large storm that hit Sydney the previous Monday, there weren't many blossoms left, but enough to get us excited and to take so many beautiful photos. This year it wasn't just UTS who attended either, all of [AnimeSydney](http://animesydney.org)  was there together! If you have never seen Japanese sakura, you should defintelly check out Auburn next year in September. I know for sure that we will be there again.
