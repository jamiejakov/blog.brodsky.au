There are hundreds of great anime opening themes songs out there. Starting with EVA and ending with the numerous Manogatari series songs. Even though I would love to mention all the [FMA Brotherhood](http://anilist.co/anime/5114/HaganenoRenkinjutsushiFullmetalAlchemist) OPs, the [Eden of the East](http://anilist.co/anime/5630/HigashinoEden), [Steins;Gate](http://anilist.co/anime/9253/SteinsGate), both [Nichijou](http://anilist.co/anime/10165/Nichijou) OPs, [Ping Pong](http://anilist.co/anime/20607/PingPongTheAnimation), [Love Hina](http://anilist.co/anime/189/LoveHina), [Chobits](http://anilist.co/anime/59/Chobits).... the list goes on. I am going to go with [Tatami Galaxy](http://anilist.co/anime/7785/YojouhanShinwaTaikei).

Why? Because not only is this song by Asian Kungfu Generation amazing, but the unique art style also makes this one of the most recognisable and memorable OP in anime history. Of course the show itself is rather niche and not that mainstream, but everyone who has seen it rates it highly, not just cause of the plot and the characters, but because the OP and ED are both top tier level. The chill music, together with the combination of realistic backgrounds and weird art style of the characters make this OP my favourite and I have it on the top of my playlist.
