I'd say this was one of my most successful cosplays, if not THE most. I have cosplayed a number of different characters in the past, as outlines in [this post](/posts/2014/cosplaying/ 'Cosplaying') about cosplay. But our joint NO MORE Eiga Dorobou (NO MOREжҳ з”»жіҘжЈ’) with Amy definitely tops the list.

SMASH! 2015 was held on the 8th and 9th of August 2015 and so we decided that I would go as the camera guy for both days. I can say for a fact, that the people loved the cosplay. We had probably over 100 photos taken in those 2 days. People were excited to see us, even though a lot of them had no idea what the cameraman or policeman were, or where we are from. Thanks to the Gintama Movie some people knew the cameraman from that, but only a handful actually recognised us as the characters from the [stop movie piracy ad](https://youtu.be/9HdCXYia-Yw?t=31), played in Japan before every movie. It was a very fun experience, now lets think of what we can cosplay as next!
